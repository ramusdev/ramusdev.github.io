>Factory

Map name: Factory
Game mode: Subversion
Location: Balkans
The "Factory" map for the "Demolition" mode is an old and once dynamically developing factory. On the map, the fighters will have to fight in the difficult conditions of the industrial zone. The map is large, replete with long sections that are shot through by snipers, but it also has narrow alleys.

The task of the attacking team is to destroy strategically important objects in the territory by penetrating the object from the eastern side and laying explosives at one of the key points.

The task of the defenders is to prepare for the invasion and prevent the destruction of the points by eliminating the enemy team or clearing a bomb.
