Forest

Map name: Forest
Game mode: Meat grinder
Location: Balkans
They say, " one in the field is not a warrior." But in the forest, one is worth an army! The "Forest" map gives you the opportunity to prove this not only to yourself, but also to others.

This is an open area with many objects: buildings, containers, a bridge, and damaged equipment.

A true warrior will be able to demonstrate his skills even in such difficult conditions.
