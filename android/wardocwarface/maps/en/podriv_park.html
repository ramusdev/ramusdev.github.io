Trailer Park

Map name: Trailer Park
Game mode: Subversion
Location: Nevada
The trailer park map for demolition mode is a place where the battle takes place near the airport, located in Nevada, in the middle of residential buildings and vans.

The task of the attacking team is to destroy strategically important objects in the territory by entering the palace from the south side and planting explosives at one of the key points. The task of the defenders is to prepare for the invasion and prevent the destruction of the points by eliminating the enemy team or clearing a bomb.
