Alleys

Map name: Alleys
Game mode: Subversion
Location: Middle East
The "Alleys" map for the "Demolition" mode represents a village in the Middle East region. Wide, but at the same time winding streets, a large number of detours, roofs and places for ambushes give players a lot of tactical opportunities.

The task of the attacking team is to destroy strategically important objects in the territory by entering the village from the north side and laying explosives at one of the key points.

The task of the defenders is to prepare for the invasion and prevent the destruction of the points by eliminating the enemy team or clearing a bomb.
