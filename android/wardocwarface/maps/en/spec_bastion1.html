Mission takes place in the Siberian mountains. Our satellites detected a strange signal distortion in the area. Contact was lost with the whole plot of mountainous terrain. All our efforts to break through the defense failed — it seems necessary to carry out the operation on the ground!

Warface squad is sent on a mission in a rather unusual environmental conditions. Expected severe weather conditions with low visibility. But we will not have time for admiring that whatever was hiding Blackwood in this cold Siberian forest to get to this will not be easy.

According to the intelligence unit on a mission to crush the resistance at three different locations to uncover a closely guarded secret. The enemy awaits our arrival and will certainly be armed with: expect meetings with elite soldiers, perhaps — and with a new opponents for us.

The cold and the unknown are the main danger. You need to be prepared for a fierce battle!

The player's task: to conduct reconnaissance of the region hidden from our satellites. On the job allowed the men, aged 25 and rank.

Terrain

Warface soldiers to fight in the cold conditions of Siberia, the snowy mountains and the forests, in old fortresses. Opponents will constantly suddenly appear from behind the trees, hide behind a snowdrift, to surround you and come from behind.

Constantly need to look down. Around every corner waiting for enemy mine, which will not leave a chance even the most experienced soldier.

Also pay attention to the red explosive barrels. Shooting at them can cause an explosion, which can hurt the enemies and your team members.

Still have the green barrels filled with deadly gas. They also are no less dangerous.
