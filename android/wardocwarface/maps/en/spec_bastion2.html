Snow Bastion - Ambush

Map Name: Snow Bastion - Ambush
Game Mode: Operation
Venue: Siberia
Entrance fee: 1 token access
Difficulty level: No
Landing in the Siberian area of the operation was successful. Warface squad managed to infiltrate the carefully guarded territory, stay there and run the "worm" in the internal network object to disable blocking the installation of Blackwood.

After a brief respite will have to continue path.

The Blackwood squads will try to blow up generators to ensure the operation of the network and to destroy the virus. You must take a position and to protect food sources and prevent their disconnection.

When the main task appears — the scale with the strength of the terminal Blackwood, who will have to defend. The indicator is located above the health bar and armor of the player. If necessary, use the resources to fix the terminal.

Try to move as far as possible, but be careful: the soldiers of the Corporation know about the presence of Warface and will be fully prepared.

According to new data, the Blackwood offers the latest combat helicopter, which can be extremely dangerous

Interaction with objects

During the passage of a special operation you can interact with some objects in the levels. Among them:

Mines

Enemy mines have a red light, min allies — green.

Installation cost — 50 resources.

Explosive Barrels

Shooting at them can cause an explosion, which can hurt the enemies and your team members.

The setup costs 100 resources.

Ladder

Ladders allow you to climb the walls of the fortress, which offers a good position for defense.

The setup costs 1000 resources.

Barricade

Barricades are perfect to protect from the enemy, not letting them pass. You can also use barricades set Blackwood.

Installation cost — 400 resources.

Strike

To call an airstrike, you need to build the stairs and climb the fence. There you will find a special case that needs to be activated. Airstrike gives excellent support and deals massive damage.

The cost of the airstrike - 300 resources.
