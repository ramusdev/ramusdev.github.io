White shark

Map Name: White shark
Game Mode: Operation
Venue: Europe
Entrance fee: 1 token access
Difficulty level: No
White shark — operation, added to the game with the November 2013 update.

Warface is fighting against the forces of Blackwood in several hot spots in the world. The Corporation leaves no plans for world domination and conducts subversive activities in different regions, forcing you to answer calls again and again.

The scouts established the location of the headquarters of the organization of Blackwood and one of its senior leaders — Oberon white. The boss of the group took refuge on the top floor of a protected building.

The player's task: to break into a closely guarded skyscraper, climb to the top, encountering on each floor fierce resistance, and eliminate Oberon white.

On the job soldiers are allowed a minimum of 25 grade.

Task

Warface soldiers have to face the forces of Blackwood in their skyscraper, which consists of 20 floors.

The building has the shape of the body of the shark. The shark is not just a predator, it is also the image of the man squad Warface will be eliminated.

On each floor of a skyscraper fighting situation changes. Will have to act according to the situation, thinking up tactics on the go. To survive in this battle — already a considerable achievement. Only the most skilled teams strong enough to beat all the levels and emerge victorious from this battle.

The skyscraper is a huge black tree — a symbol of Blackwood Corporation. The tree grows from the first floor and is holding on to its branches each subsequent floor of a skyscraper. The higher the character climbs, the harder he falls.

The assault begins with a reception and continues at each of the 19 floors of a skyscraper. The final battle takes place on the helipad at the very top of the building.

Squad Warface moves between floors on a cylindrical Elevator with glass walls. On certain floors in the Elevator embarks on a deadly gas, so be it permanently impossible: the squad is forced to go outside.

Interior

The headquarters of the Blackwood is not a storehouse of weapons, and a large modern office.

The design of the office meets the latest trends. On each floor opposite the Elevator entrance set administrative Desk, which is good for hiding.

The partition separating the office space, can be used to protect in a firefight. But note that they are destroyed after receiving a certain amount of damage. Also as cover, you can use the sofas, beds and columns.

On the floors there are many exotic plants and original interior solutions.

Be careful, in addition to other objects on the floor you can meet fuel containers that explode on contact with them. They can hurt your teammates and damage enemies.
