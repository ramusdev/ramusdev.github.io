Pyramid

Map Name: Pyramid
Game Mode: Undermining
Venue: Africa
The map is "Pyramid" mode "Undermining" is an ancient architectural complex. On the territory of the complex there are powerful statues of ancient gods, columned halls, etc. is the Central hall in the heart of the complex, gives a great advantage for the rapid distribution of tactical points in the capture and defense.

The presence of a small construction trailers demonstrates the reconstruction of certain areas of the complex.

The task of the attacking team is to destroy a strategically important facilities on the territory of the complex, entering it on the South side and laying the explosives in one of the key points.

The task of the defender is to prepare for the invasion and prevent destruction of the complex, eliminating the enemy team or razminirovanie the bomb.
