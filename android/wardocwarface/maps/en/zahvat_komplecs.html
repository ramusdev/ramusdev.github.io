The Complex

Map Name: Complex
Game Mode: Capture
Venue: Siberia
The battle on the map "Complex" going on in the engineering enterprise. You have to fight the complex, located in the Siberian mountains. The battle will go both outdoors and indoors, including in the main production hall where there is an unfinished rocket engine.

The task of the attacking team is to capture the briefcase with the secret launch codes of the missiles penetrated into the territory of the engineering complex. The task of the defender is to prepare for the invasion and to prevent the seizure of the case, eliminating the enemy team.
