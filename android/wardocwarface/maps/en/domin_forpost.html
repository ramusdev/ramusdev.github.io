Outpost

Map Name: Outpost
Game Mode: Domination
Venue: Siberia
Our task now is to achieve success in Siberia at any cost. Fortress Blackwood almost fell: our advanced troops stormed the impregnable base day and night. But only "attacks in a forehead" here are a few.

We established the location of the Outpost with several command posts of the enemy, located near the headquarters of the Blackwood. As far as we know, this is the control of the enemy's defensive systems. Capture these control points will give us a serious advantage.

Have to determine which ones to grab first. Remember that control of any two points will give us victory.

The fight will take place in the harsh winter conditions. To move between the objects as possible on open areas and trenches, if you do not want the enemy saw you.

As cover when shooting, use of abandoned military vehicles and technical installations.
