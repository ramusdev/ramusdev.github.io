Lighthouse

Map Name: Lighthouse
Game Mode: Destruction
Venue: Balkans
Map "the lighthouse" for the "Destruction" is a port with a lighthouse. With beacon, the highest point of the map and favorite spot of all the snipers, offers a beautiful view on the point of broadcast, but they should be wary of in the heat of battle it is easy not to notice the enemy creeping toward you from behind. br>
The fight will have to conduct under heavy enemy fire in harsh conditions. The map is almost symmetric and the chances of the combatants are equal. To win this mode you have three times to strike from the air on enemy positions.

To call an airstrike, you must keep control of the radio receiving station located in the center of the map.
