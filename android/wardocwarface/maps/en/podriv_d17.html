Object D17

Map name: Object D17
Game mode: Subversion
Location: Europe
The map" Object D17 "for the" Demolition " mode is a small site of an oil refining complex, with oil tanks and technical buildings. The territory of the complex is filled with all sorts of containers and specialized equipment, which turned this patch of land into a deadly maze for the fighters of both teams.

The map is average, full of narrow alleys, and there are long sections that are shot through by snipers. The task of the attacking team is to destroy strategically important objects in the territory by entering the complex from the southern side and placing explosives at one of the key points.

The task of the defenders is to prepare for the invasion and prevent the destruction of this complex by eliminating the enemy team or clearing a bomb.
