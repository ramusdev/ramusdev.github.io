Depot

Map name: Depot
Game mode: Meat grinder
Location: Balkans
"Depot" - a small-sized map that gives you the opportunity to plunge into a ruthless battle of all with all.

Sow death among the many rusty railway cars and small buildings.

But do not forget that the simplicity of this map is deceptive, there are many positions with invisible approaches, so try to look around more often.
