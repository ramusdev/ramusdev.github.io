Shapito

Map name: Header
Game mode: Team battle
Location: Circus
The usual "Oil depot" will turn into a camp of a circus troupe during the holiday. Be careful and look around more often, and when you hear the clacking of teeth-do not turn around: a predator from the depths of the sea is nearby!





Remember about the main task: to neutralize as many opponents as possible, working in a team.
