Residence

Map Name: Residence
Game Mode: Blitz
Venue: Balkans
Map "Residence" mode "Blitz" is a Oberon white residence located in the forest. In one of the rooms of the residence set the explosives. Fighting area is large enough: a lot of passes, descents in cellars and lifts to the upper floors, narrow circuitous routes and open spaces.

Developer's Video



To the main point, there are several passages in which the enemy tries to get to the explosives. This is a great place for an ambush! Pay special attention to the key points — try to take them before enemy, it will help turn the tide in your direction.

The task of the attacking team — to prevent the explosion of a charge already laid at the site.

The task of the defenders is to protect the explosives from abuse, eliminating the enemy team.
