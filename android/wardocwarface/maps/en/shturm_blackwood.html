The Blackwood Base map is a real breakthrough! You can expect a change of geographical location, excellent "readability" of players on the ground, impressive detail of objects and their customization depending on the side on which you are fighting.



"Blackwood Base" has a unique charm and color that perfectly combines with the classic gameplay of the mode.





The mechanics of opening checkpoints are unchanged: the capture of one command post leads to the opening of the next. Players will certainly be able to implement their favorite style of play on the map and get the most out of it.



At the base, you will find a harsh coastal landscape, incredible beauty of icy underground caves, a cluster of equipment and nondescript military buildings in which it is easy to hide. Base it is securely fortified and has three key points to storm which you will have to go.
