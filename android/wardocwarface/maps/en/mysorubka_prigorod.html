Suburb

Map name: Suburb
Game mode: Meat grinder
Location: Middle East
Once a picturesque "Suburb" overnight turns into a place of fierce battle in the "Meat Grinder" mode, where you will have no allies or friends.

Get ready for a short, but furious shootout that will start to break out on one end of the map, then on the other. In the center of the map is a tall building, on the second floor of which you can take a convenient position for firing.

Just do not expect that the enemies will allow you to sit there quietly.
