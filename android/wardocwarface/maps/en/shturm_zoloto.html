Black gold

Map Name: Black gold
Game Mode: Storm
Venue: Middle East
Map "Black gold" for the mode "Assault" is a rich oil-producing complex in the middle East.

At the base there are buildings, rusted cars, towers, pipes, sucker rod pumps, containers, boxes and the like. The fight will have to conduct under heavy enemy fire in harsh conditions.

The task of the attacking team is to capture command posts of the enemy and move the war front forward. The task of the defender - not to capture them. Time will show whose flag will rise above this grim place.
