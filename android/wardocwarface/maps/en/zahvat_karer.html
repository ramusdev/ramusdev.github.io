Careers

Map Name: Careers
Game Mode: Capture
Venue: North America
Map "Quarry" for the "Capture" mode is a quarry mining facility. On-site career is all about specifics place of getting mine ores, concrete blocks, construction equipment, construction plant.

Approaches to building crowded with massive metonymy blocks. The abundance of positions for snipers and surprise attacks will allow us to develop and use dozens of tactics and strategies.

The task of the attacking team is to capture the briefcase with the secret codes to launch missiles. The task of the defender is to prepare for the invasion and to prevent the seizure of the case, eliminating the enemy team.
