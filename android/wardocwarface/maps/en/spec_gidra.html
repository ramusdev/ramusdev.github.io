Terminal

In "Hydra", players will defend a special terminal in the center of the map from opponents, each wave of which becomes more dangerous than the previous one. The enemy will want to regain control of the weapon by disabling it.



When completing the main task, a scale indicator will appear with the strength of the Blackwood terminal, which will need to be protected. The indicator is located above the player's health and armor scale. Use resources to fix the terminal, if necessary.

However, remember — this is a complex device and you will not be able to repair it more than once.

Resources

Throughout the entire special operation, you will be able to receive Arenacoins. Resources for destroyed waves of enemies, for special combat successes, and from special chests.



Using the earned resources, you will be able to purchase grenade launchers, defensive turrets, laser and ballistic shields, which will significantly simplify the defense of the terminal and the destruction of the enemy.

Random events

On the map, events such as a power outage of auxiliary vehicles, an increase in the number of attackers, as well as the use of force barriers, poison gas, or smoke screens by the enemy can occur.

This means that you will not be able to use the same tactics and will have to adapt on the go!
