Black Mamba

Map Name: Black Mamba
Game Mode: Storm
Venue: Africa
Map "Black Mamba" mode "Assault" is a missile system located in Africa. Rocket kit is a control center with which you can disable communication of the radar and to open the gate in the main bunker and control room, with its help you can get direct access to a missile silo and control room the launch.

Please pay special attention to the construction crane, which can be used for a sudden attack, and the ventilation shaft: it is possible to go behind enemy lines and to surprise.

The third key point of the complex and the control gate of the mine and the rocket launch. Focus on location and skillfully use the shelter and the side aisles, it can become a serious advantage. But beware: the warm-up phase, the rocket engines will incinerate everything that will be in dangerous proximity.
