Market

Map name: Market
Game mode: Team battle
Location: Middle East
The "Market" map for the "Team Battle" mode is a battle in the central market. On the territory you will find pavilions, warehouses with things, shops, technical facilities. Judging by the map, there are several key places on the square, taking which you can get favorable positions for shooting.

There are also good points for snipers, but in general the market consists of many moves and is ideal for ambushing or laying mines. Be extremely careful, danger can be waiting around every corner!
