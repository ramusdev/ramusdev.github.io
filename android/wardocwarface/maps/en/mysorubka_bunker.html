Game Mode: Chopper

Bunker — games map PvP-mode "meat Grinder." Events take place in the rocket complex, located in Africa.

Interesting map for battlefield fans any class. Here are long corridors and narrow passages, small rooms and open halls. Moreover, it is a multistory map, which ensures the variety of possible tactical decisions. Recommend you often to look around and to listen to suspicious sounds nearby. And, of course, try to launch a rocket!

Here each fighter needs to stand up for itself and fight against all alone. The task of every soldier — to score the most points.
