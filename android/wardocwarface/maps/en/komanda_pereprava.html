Ferry

Map name: Ferry
Game mode: Team battle
Location: South America
"Ferry" - a map for the "Team Battle" mode. The map is open, which makes it a very interesting place to fight.

Snipers of the enemy team will surely control the surrounding area from the hills. The bridge and the river are perfectly shot through.

There are also several dilapidated buildings on the map that can serve as a shelter for both you and the enemy fighters. To survive, be careful and avoid open spaces.
