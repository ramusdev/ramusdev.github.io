Dangerous experiment — operation, added to the game in October 2015.

Scientists Blackwood arranged experiments with cybernetic implants in a secret laboratory. For everyone it was quite obvious that such experiments are dangerous, but that didn't stop our opponents. And there was something to be expected — the research is out of control.

As a result, a region in Eastern Europe is awash with the victims of failed experiments. Thousands of cybernetic zombies are on the loose, and their number is increasing. According to the intelligence unit on a mission to suppress the crowd of escaped mutants to fully clean up the area.

Blackwood is unable to resist such an influx of monsters, so expect their units was not worth it. However, they conducted experiments on the creation of several types of cybermutt, some of which are much more dangerous than mercenaries Blackwood.

In the darkness hides a deadly danger — you need to be ready for anything!

Task

Squad Warface must accompany machine equipped with a generator AMY, to the lab that produces the zombie-cyborgs. The fact that the EMP pulse disables electronic and cybernetic systems, and a powerful EMP blast and not have to cause irreparable damage to the plants that produce monsters. Thus, the release of new zombie will stop.

In addition to the final "flash" in the lair of the enemy, for which the operation is conducted, from time to time AMY is triggered during the movement of the car, destroying implants enemies. Then all the enemies near the car die, dead companions rise, and all players recovering health, armor and ammunition. However, the accumulation of energy for the EMP blast takes time, so don't expect frequent triggering of this "magic wand".



During the mission the soldiers will reflect some serious zombie attacks, which aim to destroy followed by transport. No, AMY will not be able to do the job, so you need to do everything possible to not let anyone to the car.

Battles in the RAID are in various conditions. It all starts in the forest on the outskirts of the lab, this detachment Warface attack the weakest opponents.

As you move forward through the swamp, and changing the setting — the soldiers first reach the repair station, where the machine completely restores the strength, and then enter into a long road tunnel. By that time, the squad is attacked by more than 5 types of monsters.

The journey ends in the laboratory, Blackwood battles from 8 types of enemies and the Destroyer, the boss of the RAID.

Levels of difficulty

The operation can be completed in three types of difficulty — "Easy", "Hard" and "Pro". The scenario of events in all jobs the same, but quite different the difficulty of their passing, mainly due to increasing damage to enemies.

The first difficulty is the simplest. Access to it is opened already on the fifth rank. In fact, this trial mode to pass which is not difficult, even for the unprepared squad. Think of it as a tour of the mission, just watch and remember the sequence of events.

The level of "Difficult" is a slightly difficult mission, "Easily", but here special weapons "Antizombi", inflicting increased damage. Access mission opens at rank 5.

But the mission of "Pros" a challenge to the strongest and most coordinated groups. The complexity of this task is that two or three zombies are able to "chew" a car with a generator in a matter of seconds, respectively, on a mandatory basis, all enemies should be shot on the approach to the car. To undergo a special operation at this level of complexity can not everyone. Access to "Pro" requires 10 grade and passed the "Dangerous experiment" in the mode "Hard".

Enemies

According to intelligence reports, cybermutt well armed, so they are extremely dangerous. Blackwood scientists were developing at once to create multiple types of mutants, each of which is attributed to its name: the Prototype, omega, Sigma, Kamikaze, Screamer, Bully, Chimera and Demolisher. Each has a different and carries a different potential threat.
