Hangar

Map name: Hangar
Game mode: Team battle
Location: Nevada
The "Air Base" map for the "Team Battle" mode is a battle on the territory of a civil aviation base. The team battle will take place on the territory of the civil aviation base-both around the hangar and in it. The abundance of entry points to the building is very convenient for attacking groups — there will always be an opportunity to bypass the opponent and suddenly attack him.

If you prefer to immediately reduce the distance with the enemy, there is a place for such a maneuver — for example, you can shoot inside the liner located in the hangar! And especially risky "hotheads" can even try to start the engines of the plane!
