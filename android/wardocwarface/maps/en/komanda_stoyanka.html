Parking lot

Map name: Parking lot
Game mode: Team battle
Location: America
The "Parking Lot" map for the "Team Battle" mode is a battle in the conditions of a typical American suburban development. On the territory you will find residential one-story houses, trailers, hangars, technical structures, sparse desert vegetation. Two things are of particular interest.

First — planes sometimes take off from a nearby airfield. They raise a real dust storm in the town, because of which visibility is reduced to almost zero. Most likely, you will have to seek shelter for a while until the sand settles.

Second — in the center of the village is a giant SUV, which you can jump on and sometimes pick up a grenade launcher there.
