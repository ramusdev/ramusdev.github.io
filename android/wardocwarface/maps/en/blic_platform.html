Ocean

Map name: Ocean
Game mode: Blitz
Location: Atlantic Ocean
The "Platform" map for the "Blitz" mode is an oil-producing two-story oil-producing platform located in the Atlantic Ocean off the southern coast of Argentina. When developing the "Platform", all the past experience of creating PvP maps was taken into account.

The level is made in contrasting red and black industrial colors — so it is easier to distinguish between opponents. The design of the map is made extremely simple, so that players can get used to it in 2-3 matches.

The task of the attacking team is to prevent the explosion of a charge already laid on the territory of the complex.

The task of the defenders is to protect the explosives from encroachments by eliminating the enemy team.
