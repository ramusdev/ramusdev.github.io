Quarter

Map name: Quarter
Game mode: Team battle
Location: Nevada
The "Quarter" map for the "Team Battle" mode is a battle on the territory of a residential sector of a provincial town in Nevada.

The team battle will take place on the streets of the block, mostly built up with one-story houses.

Use the backyards to get around the enemies from the rear, and do not forget to mine the approaches to your position if you have to defend yourself. There are a lot of abandoned cars left on the road, which can serve as a good shelter for those who are ready to go ahead.
