Mojave

Map name: Mojave
Game mode: Battle Royale
Location: Nevada
The "Mojave" map for the "Battle Royale" mode is a military base located in the Mojave Desert.

On this map, you will find an abandoned city enveloping you, ominous sounds and a sense of constant danger, as an enemy can appear from any corner.

Your task is to eliminate the opponents and remain the last survivor. It will not be easy, because the game will start without firearms.
