Destination

Map Name: Destination
Game Mode: Undermining
Venue: Asia
Map "final destination" mode "Undermining" is a roof of a skyscraper. On the roof there is a lot of medium-sized objects like a flower greenhouse, air ducts, ventilation shafts and the like. Between radio towers helipad.

The average map, full of tight alleys, where there are long sections that sweep snipers.

The task of the attacking team is to destroy a strategically important facilities on the territory, penetrated the roof on the East side and laying the explosives in one of the key points. The task of the defender is to prepare for the invasion and prevent destruction of radio transmitters, eliminating the enemy team or razminirovanie the bomb.
