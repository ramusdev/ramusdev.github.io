Compact machine, designed in 1998 based on the assault rifle SG 550. The barrel and gas tube shortened to 226 mm, and the sealing frame is integrated with the piston rod in a single movement.

Purchase

SIG 552 can be purchased in the game store ("Weapon") as for the loans forever, and Warbucks for a long time (7 or 30 days).
