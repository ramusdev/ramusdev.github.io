Historical note

Bushmaster Adaptive Combat Rifle (ACR) SPR — originally developed by Magpul Industries and is focused primarily on the civilian and police markets. There is no doubt that the concept of rifles MAGPUL Masada/Bushmaster ACR had an impact army weapons system SCAR.

The rifle has a modular design, which is based on receiver-aluminum alloy. Below it with pins attached polymer housing of the trigger, combined with the receiver and pistol grip. Quick-change guns.

Gas operated automatics with a manual regulator, gas piston with short stroke and the rod-pusher is installed on each separate barrel and at partial disassembly of the barrel is a separate module.

Rifle can be equipped with butts of different types — folding and telescoping side adjustable or awkward ("sniper").
