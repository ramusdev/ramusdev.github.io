Historical note

M16 Custom SPR — a semi-automatic sniper rifle, developed under the concept of the use by the U.S. army infantry snipers, used for observation of the battlefield, intelligence priorities, distribution of data means targeting fire support, working in the interests of the unit as well as for direct support of the infantry beyond the range of your normal shooters.

The concept was adopted by many Western armies in 1990-ies under the influence of the Soviet experience, to develop and implement this concept in the 1960-ies.

The rifle is based on the latest at the time of modification of M16A4, the regular weapon of the U.S. marine corps. In M16 SPR Custom used the main components of the M16A4 rifle, in particular a standard receiver and barrel are made of stainless steel.
