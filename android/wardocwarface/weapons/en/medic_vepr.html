Historical note

Carbine Vepr-12 was designed at the factory "Sickle" as an analogue of the widely distributed series shotguns "saiga". Its a niche sport, but also a "boar" is used by police forces and private security companies.

The basis of the shotgun lies proven design Kalashnikov machine gun (PKK), modified taking into account the needs of the sport and civil use. Many of the fixtures became much easier - in particular, the two-way fuse.

The shotgun is available in three versions that differ only in the length of the barrel and a metal stock frame, folding sideways. From the market model with a shortened barrel added lock function firing when folded, which significantly increases security.

Boar, compared to the "Saiga‐12", has a small recoil that allows to use it even people with weak physical training. Additionally has a guide strip that allows mounting on various types of rifle sights. It is acceptable to install the flame arrester and muffler.
