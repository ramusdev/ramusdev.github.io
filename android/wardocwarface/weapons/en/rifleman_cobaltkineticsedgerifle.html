A lightweight assault rifle manufactured by Cobalt Kinetics, the receiver of which is made of a single piece of aluminum.

Combat Effectiveness

A stormtrooper weapon with impressive damage and shooting accuracy. This model is effective at almost all distances and is able to hit the target with a single hit to the head.

The weapon will be an excellent assistant in solving tactical tasks of any complexity due to the variability of the use of 5 unique modules.



Advantages

- High damage
- Easily controlled recoil
- Three modification slots with unique modules

Disadvantages

- Average accuracy when shooting from the hip

Historical background

The Cobalt Kinetics Stealth Pistol and the Cobalt Kinetics EDGE Rifle are developed by Cobalt Kinetics, a company that specializes in creating high-precision weapons in a unique design using advanced engineering solutions.

The Cobalt Kinetics EDGE Rifle is a version of the popular American AR-15 platform. The weapon uses the company's own development-the Dual Drop mechanism, which guarantees ease of use.

It allows for quick reloading due to the presence of a second front control lever on the left side of the receiver, when pressed, which releases the bolt.

In addition, the weapon has a non-standard barrel of increased strength, resistant to heat and corrosion, which makes the Cobalt Kinetics EDGE Rifle truly reliable.
