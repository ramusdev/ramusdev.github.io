Historical note

The Kalashnikov (AK) 9 is a submachine gun created based on the AK-74 is a shortened and lightweight version of this machine. In addition, it is a rare weapon available to the engineer class.

The main difference between AK-9 from Kalashnikov rifles – a shortened gas operated mechanism and the barrel. Another difference is a new pistol grip with better ergonomics. In addition, on the Shank the bottom strap attached, Piccatiny for mounting flashlight, laser pointer or handle.

Now the AK-9 is actively used by special units of the Armed forces and the interior Ministry, replacing the outdated "as VAL", "9A-91" and "CP-3 "Whirlwind".
