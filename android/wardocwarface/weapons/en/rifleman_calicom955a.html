Historical note

CALICO M955A - universal automatic rifle blowback that uses cartridges of .22 LR, or 9 × 19mm Parabellum. Manufactured by Calico Light Weapons Systems in Hillsboro, Oregon. The prototype rifle is a Calico M950/950A, semi-automatic submachine gun without the stock.

The gun has many modifications that will turn it into almost a full carbine, including - Calico M955A magazine with 100 rounds of ammunition.
