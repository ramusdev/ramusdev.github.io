HCAR is interesting both for snipers who want to go beyond the usual gameplay, and for stormtroopers who want to try something different from their usual machine guns.

Acquisition

Weapons can be obtained both temporarily and permanently for credits.

Combat Effectiveness

The HCAR's gameplay features are something new for Warface, as it is the game's first semi-automatic assault rifle. HCAR deals double damage to the body and limbs. In fact, the weapon has a damage of 160 units, and the display on the comparative lines of the TTX is left so as not to break the general perception of assault rifles.

High damage is compensated by a noticeable recoil and a magazine for only 20 rounds.

Advantages

- Excellent damage
- Excellent range
- Excellent rate of fire
- Excellent accuracy in aiming mode
- Three slots of modifications with the entire range of equipment for the attack aircraft
- High penetration ability.

Disadvantages

- Small store capacity
- Strong recoil

Historical background

The HCAR assault rifle, created by the American company Ohio Ordnance Works, was presented to the public relatively recently, in 2013. But everything new is well-forgotten old. It is based on the legendary BAR M1918 machine gun, which was adopted more than a century ago and participated in two world wars.



All the past century, progress has not stood still. From the famous ancestor, the HCAR differs in almost half the weight, the ability to install a muzzle brake, modern butts and sights, but at the same time the caliber has been preserved .30-06 406-mm barrel is covered with oval depressions.

Their purpose is to reduce the weight and improve the cooling of the barrel by increasing its surface area. The rifle is equipped with a modern flame extinguisher, has a gas regulator, a new loading handle and a Mil-Spec telescopic stock.
