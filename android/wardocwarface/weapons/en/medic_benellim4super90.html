Historical note

In the United States for quite a long time, work was done to create a combat semi-automatic shotgun. In September 1998, a competition was announced, which was won by shotgun Benelli M4 Super 90, designed by the Italian firm "Benelli Armi Spa". Shotgun was adopted by the marine Corps of the United States under the symbol М1014.

Tehnicheski "Benelli M4 Super 90" is a smooth-bore self-loading shotgun with a magazine for 6 rounds. Recharge is due to the exhaust gases in a two gas cylinder, symmetrically located under the barrel for greater reliability.

This system patented by the company "Benelli Armi Spa" and has the name "AutoRegulatingGasOperated" (ARGO), which translated into Russian means "self-regulating gas-operated". The chamber implemented for ammunition with a length of 76 mm (3 inches), but the above mechanism allows you to work with cartridges with a length of 70 mm (2.75 inches).

The bore of a shotgun chrome-plated, locking occurs due to the rotating bolt on the two lugs. The outer surface of the barrel and all metal parts are protected from wear by a special coating.

Pistol grip, handguard and buttstock made of high quality plastic. The cover of the receiver is equipped with a strap, Pikattini that allows you to set the arms collimator, night and other sights.
