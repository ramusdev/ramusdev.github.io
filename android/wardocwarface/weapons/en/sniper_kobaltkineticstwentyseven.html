The Cobalt Kinetics Twenty-Seven is a sniper rifle with the ability to quickly aim, as well as one of the highest accuracy rates when shooting from the hip among semi-automatic rifles.

Combat Effectiveness

A new development from Cobalt Kinetics that combines the charm of a classic hunting rifle with the convenience of a modern semi-automatic rifle. The telescopic stock can be adjusted at any time according to the anatomical data of the shooter.

Historical background

Another development of the American company Cobalt Kinetics, which is a modern analogue of traditional semi-automatic rifles. The model is designed on the AR-15 platform using light and heavy-duty materials.

The sniper rifle has a unique design of the lower part of the receiver and the box, which makes it universal: the shooter gets the ability to adjust to individual anatomical features.

This high-precision model provides great opportunities for creating a wide variety of configurations for a specific combat case.
