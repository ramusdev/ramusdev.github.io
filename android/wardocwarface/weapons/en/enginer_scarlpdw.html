Historical note

SCAR-L PDW (eng. Special Operations Forces Combat Assault Rifle — Light Personal Defense Weapon) short barrel machine, developed by the American division of the Belgian company "FN Herstal" in 2005 and produced by "FNH USA Inc.".

In 2003, CSR USA (USSOCOM) published requirements for a new modular assault rifle SCAR. The new weapon was intended to replace American forces obsolete M4A1 carbines, M16 assault rifle, M14 and SR-25. In December 2004, the results of the competition, the winner was declared, the weapons system of the company "FN Herstal".



Automation works due to the vapor engine with a short stroke gas piston. Locking barrel by turning the shutter 3 lugs. Firing from a closed bolt. On the front side of the gas chamber rotary switch mounted gas regulator.

Slide the l-shaped top has an elongation, the front wall of which abuts the gas piston. USM hammer type, made in the form of a single module and can fire single shots or continuous bursts.

The barrel is removable, quick-change. The barrel mount is done with 6 screws, and its replacement requires only one tool and takes only a few minutes. The front of the barrel has a slotted flash suppressor is removable, allowing you to install on top of it quick-detachable silencer.

Receiver is made of two halves: the top is aluminum, containing in itself the action Assembly with the stem, and the lower plastic, which is a pistol grip with the controls, the receiver will store the USM module. Connected the two halves with transverse pin located in the front part of the lower half.

Arm cocking bolt is removable and can be installed on either side of the arms. The handle is attached directly to the bolt carrier and moves when shooting with her. Before a slit for the arm on both sides of the arms there are holes for cooling the barrel, shutting off the extended front part of the bolt.

On top of the receiver and on the bottom and the sides of the forearm there is a guide type Picatinny rail, allowing to put any collimator and optical sights with corresponding mounts and different arms.

Regular SCAR-L PDW is equipped with a detachable sighting devices, including folding adjustable rear sight mounted on the rail, and an adjustable folding front sight in the ring namushnike application installed on a gas chamber.

The butt and pistol grip made of durable plastic. The butt of a simplified, fixed and fitted with an adjustable cheek. On the back of the butt has a thick rubber recoil pad and sling swivel for attaching a gun belt. Also swivels are on both sides of the receiver, front and back.

The power of the gun stock is made from double-row magazine for 30 rounds. In SCAR-L PDW can be used stores of rifles such as M16 or AR-15. To power the weapon uses cartridges 5,56×45 mm NATO.
