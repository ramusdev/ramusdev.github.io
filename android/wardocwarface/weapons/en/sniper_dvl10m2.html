Lobaev Arms tactical sniper rifle, codenamed "Urbana". It is a modification of the DVL-10, specialized for fighting in the city.

Combat Effectiveness

DVL-10 M2 is a direct competitor to the SV-98, covering all the disadvantages of the second. The rifle has a number of unique modules: bipods, which increase the speed of changing weapons; flame extinguisher and muzzle brake, which reduce the minimum spread and recoil, respectively; sight, which increases the speed of entry into the sight by 25%, but also reduces the exit speed by 50%.



In addition, it ignores the protection from a single hit.

Advantages

- Ignores bulletproof vests with one-shot protection
- High damage
- High damage multipliers in the limb
- High body damage multiplier
- Fast aiming speed
- High minimum damage

Disadvantages

- You can't install a silencer

Historical background

The DVL-10 M2 light tactical sniper rifle is an advanced development of the Russian company Lobaev Arms and can be successfully used wherever compactness, mobility and high accuracy are needed.
