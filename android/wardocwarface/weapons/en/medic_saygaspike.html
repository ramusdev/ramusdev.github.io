A modified version of the" Saiga " in the bullpup layout. The gun is manufactured by Center Balanced Systems. The weapon is equipped with heavy bullet cartridges, which allows you to deal high damage and knock down.

Combat Effectiveness

Saiga Spike is made in a very simple and concise design. The layout of the bull-pup has a positive effect on the accuracy of the weapon, and the Picatinny bars allow you to install all the modules available to medics.

But the most interesting thing about this shotgun concerns its characteristics. The Saiga Spike is loaded with a bullet cartridge, not shot. The power of the shot is such that at a distance of up to 12 meters, it knocks over any opponent on his back, if you can not kill him with the first hit.

The damage of 500 units and one bullet does not leave a chance for opponents at close and medium range, but at the same time, a small ammunition and a low rate of fire do not give the right to make a mistake.

Advantages

- Excellent shooting accuracy in aiming mode
- Good penetration ability
- Two slots of modifications with a large range of equipment

Disadvantages

- Low rate of fire
- Small ammunition

Historical background

The adjustable Spike USM was developed by the Russian company Bulletec for use in sports. The unique shape and optimal angle of the trigger make this product extremely comfortable.

The side faces of the trigger give the shooter clear information about the force, stroke and position of the finger, making the shot as predictable and accurate as possible. USM Spike is made of high-quality alloy chromium steel and heat-treated in a vacuum furnace, due to which it has a significant strength resource.

It is designed for civilian weapons based on the Kalashnikov assault rifle, including the Saiga-12.
