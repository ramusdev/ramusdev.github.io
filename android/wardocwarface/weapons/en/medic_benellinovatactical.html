Historical note

Shotgun Benelli Nova tactical was developed in the late 1990-ies by the Italian company "Benelli Armi Spa", while the goal was to create an easy, versatile, and powerful weapons using the most modern technologies. Model Benelli Nova shotgun manual, rechargeable via rolling of the forearm (pump method).

Shotgun Benelli Nova has a receiver made integral with the butt of durable plastic, with reinforcing steel inserts. Elongated longitudinally sliding forend, convenient for different variants of grip, also made of plastic. Food is from underbarrel tubular magazine, equips one patron through a window at the bottom of the receiver.

A standard magazine holds 4 rounds with a length of core 76mm ("Magnum") or 3 - length of the liner 89 mm ("supermagnum"), in the prescribed extension capacity grows to 6 rounds with a length of 76 mm or up to 7 with the sleeve 70 mm.

As indicated, the chamber allows you to use any ammo 12 gauge, up to the most powerful "supermagnum", so the design includes rubber shock-absorbing butt plate in the butt to install special devices that reduce returns.

Sights in combat variant of the Benelli Nova is a rifle type front sight in the namushnike, normal open or diopter sights. Push fuse is located in front of the trigger guard.
