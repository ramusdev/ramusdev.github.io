Historical background

The AMB-17 is a small-sized automatic rifle from the Kalashnikov Concern, created using modern technologies on the basis of the AM-17-developed by the legendary gunsmith Yevgeny Dragunov.

The letter "B" ("silent") in the designation indicates the presence of a built-in silencer. Compact, but with a solid caliber of 9x39 mm, the weapon is intended for use by the special services and the army instead of the AS "Val" and VSS "Vintorez".
