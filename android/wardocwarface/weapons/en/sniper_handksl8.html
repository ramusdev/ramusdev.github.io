Historical note

Heckler & Koch SL8 was developed by the famous German weapon company Heckler & Koch in 1998, specifically for the civilian market.

The new rifle was based on the design and technology of the army assault rifles H&K G36. H&K SL8 uses the vapor-automatic located above the barrel, gas piston with short stroke and automatic adjustment of the gas outlet. The bolt handle foldable located above the receiver and can be rotated in any direction.

The receiver and the box is made of impact-resistant plastic, and have a modular design with interchangeable receiver.

For sale in countries where restrictions on the capacity of the store civilian weapons, rifles H&K SL8 receiver are equipped for special shops with a capacity of 10 cartridges.
