Historical note

ORSIS T-5000 (from Rus. the weapon system; also ORSIS T-5000) is a high-precision shop sniper rifle with a longitudinally sliding gate. Produced arms factory "ORSIS" industrial group "survey systems", located in Moscow. Currently the rifle is only sold in a civilian version.

The rifle was presented in 2011 at the international Russian exhibition of arms in Nizhny Tagil.



In early June 2012, the Management team "A" SPC FSB won the international competition of police and army snipers (eng. Police & military sniper world cup) with the use of a rifle T-5000. In September of the same year the rifle was tested in the composition of the set of equipment of land forces "Warrior".

Design T-5000 is based on the original bolt group are made of stainless steel. Shutter longitudinal sliding, rotating. The barrel and trigger mechanism of the rifle is also made of stainless steel. The T-5000 is set to trunk ORSIS SE, obtained by single-pass cutting. Accuracy of manufacturing of the barrel of the order of 0.002 mm.

On the trunk are made valleys for reducing the time of cooling of the barrel and reduce the weight of weapons in General. The barrel muzzle is threaded for attachment of muzzle brake-compensator or other muzzle device (equipped with a three-chamber muzzle brake compensator ORSIS).

The bed of aluminum alloy, ergonomic pistol grip, adjustable buttstock, folding stock mechanism made of steel with magnetic latch in the folded position. Depending on the variant, the trigger, trigger pull can be adjusted continuously ("Hunter" — allows you to configure free running and the force from 900 to 1500 grams; "Varmint" — from 500 to 900 grams).

Three-position fuse is made in the rear of the bolt and, if necessary, allows for the manipulation of the shutter when the fuse or completely block the trigger mechanism and the shutter.

The rifle is equipped with guide type Picatinny rail for mounting an optical sight and a rail to mount preobjective nozzle. The folding buttstock is adjustable in length. On the stock installed height-adjustable podsednik.

Food ammunition is made from detachable box magazine capacity 5 and 10 rounds.

The performance characteristics of a rifle T-5000 allow you to hit targets at any time of the day or night, in all weather conditions, without pre-zeroing and technical training at distances up to 1,650 meters. Rifles demonstrated accuracy at the level of no greater than 0.5 angular minutes (11 cm to 800 m).
