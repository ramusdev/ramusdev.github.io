A self-loading rifle developed by Fabarm. It has a high rate of fire and low recoil.

Acquisition

Fabarm P. S. S. 10 can be purchased in the game store ("Kits") in boxes for warbucks, where you can get both permanently and temporarily. Also, this gun can be purchased for credits forever.

Combat Effectiveness

The shotgun has excellent characteristics: excellent damage, good range and rate of fire. Great chance to kill with the first shot. If the opponent survives (which is unlikely), the player has a chance to finish him off with a second shot.

Advantages

- Excellent rate of fire
- Good penetration ability
- Does not require opening from suppliers and repair
- Two slots of modifications with a large range of equipment

Disadvantages

- Small firing range

Historical background

The P. S. S. 10 model was developed by Fabarm specifically for the International Practical Shooting Association (IPSC) and human rights bodies. On the receiver there are "Weaver" slats, on the barrel there is an adjustable rear sight and a front sight with an insert made of optical fiber.

A special feature of this model is a ten-shot magazine (9+1).



The details of the weapon are qualitatively processed, the butt and forearm are coated with soft rubber, which increases comfort when using and eliminates the possibility of slipping the gun out of your hands.
