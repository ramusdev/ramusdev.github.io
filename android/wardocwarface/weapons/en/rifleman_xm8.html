Historical note

XM8 is an assault rifle developed jointly by the American arms company ATK (Alliant Techsystems) and the German company Heckler Koch rifle based on the HK G36. HM8 developed primarily to replace the M16 and M4A1 rifles with less weight and greater reliability.



HM8, like the HK G36, which is based on automation with a short stroke gas piston, located above the barrel, and locking by a rotating bolt with 7 lugs. The bolt handle is also available for both hands.

The receiver and forend made of durable plastic. The butt is adjustable along the length of the telescoping five-position, easily replaceable, also made of plastic.
