Historical note

JS 9mm (Jian She 9mm) submachine gun, developed in 2005 by a Chinese company "Jian She" together with the 208 research Institute of the PLA. For the first time this weapon was introduced in may 2005 at the exhibition "International Police Equipment Expo" held in Beijing. The weapons were planned to produce for export or for the Chinese police.

Automatic JS 9mm based on the principle of free movement of gate, the channel of the barrel when a shot is held against the shutter. Submachine gun built under the scheme "bullpup". The cocking lever of the shutter located on the right side.



USM provides a single shooting, all three live rounds and automatic fire. Check box to switch modes of fire is located above the pistol grip. He is also the fuse.

The gun is equipped with an automatic fuse located on the back side of the pistol grip. When covering a palm of the handle lever guard is pressed and disables the system protection.

On the muzzle of the barrel can be fastened removable silencer and flame trap.

Receiver is made from lightweight aluminum alloy and the bed and shoulder — made of impact resistant plastic.

The JS 9mm is not equipped with ordinary mechanical sighting device, in return for which the bar Picatinny rail can be installed a variety of types of collimator, optical and night sights.
