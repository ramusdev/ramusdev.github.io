Historical note

Daewoo K1 - automatic submachine gun 5.56 mm Due to the ease and reliability is still important, especially to engineer.
Daewoo K1 was created to replace the submachine gun M3 on order ROKASWC(command of special forces of the Republic of Korea). Prototypes appeared in 1980, and in 1981 "K1" was adopted by the army of South Korea and Indonesia.

The basis of the Daewoo K1 assault formed the American M16A1 rifle. The main differences Daewoo K1 — retractable stock made of wire with a plastic butt pad, the design of the forearm and sighting devices, additional mode of firing bursts with a cutoff of 3 rounds.
