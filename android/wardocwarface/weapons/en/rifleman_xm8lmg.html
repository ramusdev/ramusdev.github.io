Historical note

XM8 LMG is a light machine gun, developed jointly by the American arms company ATK (Alliant Techsystems) and the German company Heckler & Koch-based rifles H&K G36. HM8 LMG developed primarily to replace the M16 and M4A1 rifles with less weight and greater reliability.



HM8 LMG based on automation with a short stroke gas piston, located above the barrel, and locking by a rotating bolt with 7 lugs. The receiver and forend made of durable plastic.
