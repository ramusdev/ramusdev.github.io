Historical note

M249 Para — light machine gun designed and manufactured by the Belgian company "Fabrique Nationale of Herstal". Work on the development of the machine gun started in early 1960-ies and ended in 1974.

In 1982, the machine gun came in the serial production of the FN office in southern California and is produced under license in Australia, Greece and the United States.

Direct competitors of the M249 Para machine gun HK MG4 are German and Israeli IMI Negev. It is worth noting that of the three of them only HK MG4 no modifications chambered in 7.62×51 mm NATO, so it is less universal.

The M249 Para has popularity in the military industry and is in service in the armed forces of several countries, including in Belgium, USA, Canada, Australia and many others.
