Historical note

Derya MK-10 VR 102 — modern shotgun developed and produced by Turkish company "Derya International Arms". The development was conducted according to the highest standards using modern materials. This shotgun combines high firepower, tactical flexibility and easy handling.

The shotgun has a manual safety located on the left side above the pistol grip.



For the manufacture of rifles used steel, aluminum and plastic. The butt made of impact-resistant polymer with the possibility of folding on the left side.

On the Derya MK-10 VR 102 includes a rear sight, located on the carrying handle. Front sight mounted on the far end of the venting tube. That is, the sighting line is quite short and high, which justifies itself in combat arms, but not suitable for hunting if you are shooting with buckshot.
