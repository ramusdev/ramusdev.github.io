An Italian three-barrelled shotgun developed by Chiappa Firearms. Triple-barreled is the main, but not the only feature of Triple Threat. This rifle is also compact and highly ergonomic.

Combat Effectiveness

A semi-automatic rifle that deals high damage and has a high rate of fire, good accuracy when shooting from the hip and a good range. In addition, the gun deals the same damage to both the body and the limbs.

Among the disadvantages, you can select a small number of shots that you can make.

The Triple Threat flame suppressor increases the penetration ability, reduces the damage drop, but also reduces the minimum accuracy.

Advantages

- High damage
- Low time interval between shots
- Quick recharge
- High accuracy in the scope
- High damage multipliers in the body and limbs

Disadvantages

- You can only load 3 rounds

Historical background

Triple Threat - a rifle of the breaking type, introduced in 2012 by the Italian company Chiappa Firearms. The firepower of the classic double-barreled rifle was increased by the manufacturer in an unusual way — a third was added to the two horizontally positioned barrels from above.

All of them have a length of 470 mm and form an almost equilateral triangle on the cut.

Chiappa Triple Threat has another interesting feature-a removable wooden butt, thanks to which a light movement of the hand turns the gun into a legal sawn-off shotgun. Its ultra-compact size and firepower make the Chiappa Triple Threat the perfect weapon for self-defense in confined spaces.
