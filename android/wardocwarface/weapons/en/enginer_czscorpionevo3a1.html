Historical note

CZ Scorpion Evo3 A1 though is the "descendant" of the famous Scorpion vz. 61, absolutely not similar to the prototype in both appearance and design. The first presentation of a new model "Scorpio" was held in may 2009 at the exhibition IDET-2009.



The weapon passed all the necessary tests, the designers took into account all the wishes of the representatives of the army and the police, who took an active part in the creation of this gun.

Abbreviation EVO3 in the designation of the weapon says that it belongs to the third generation submachine guns called "Skorpion", A1, that is a modification of the automatic mode of fire.
