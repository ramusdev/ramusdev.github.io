Historical background

The Cobalt Kinetics Stealth Pistol and the Cobalt Kinetics EDGE Rifle are developed by Cobalt Kinetics, a company that specializes in creating high-precision weapons in a unique design using advanced engineering solutions.



The Cobalt Kinetics Stealth Pistol is a compact and balanced weapon that adapts easily to the needs of the shooter. It is designed with the expectation of improving the control and comfort of shooting-the weapon is second to none in terms of ease of use.



Like the rest of the company's models, the submachine gun has a Dual Drop mechanism that allows you to reload faster. The elements of the model have a black nitride surface, and the 9-inch barrel is made of high-strength chromium-molybdenum steel, which together provides maximum wear resistance and reliability of the assembly.
