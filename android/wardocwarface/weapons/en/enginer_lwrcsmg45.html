Combat Effectiveness

According to its gaming characteristics, the LWRC SMG-45 is an improved version of the UMP. It has the highest damage among submachine guns and kills opponents with a single hit to the head.

This is compensated by the lowest rate of fire, as well as the average firing distance and considerable recoil. In addition, the LWRC SMG-45 has 2 unique modules.

The gold version, in addition to the luxurious appearance, traditionally has slightly increased characteristics.

In addition, the LWRC SMG-45 has 2 unique modules: the LWRC SMG-45 collimator with 1.3-fold magnification and the LWRC SMG-45 angle handle: increases accuracy, significantly reduces recoil, increases the speed of changing weapons.

Historical background

The SMG-45 is a modern submachine gun from the American company LWRC. It was introduced to the public in 2016. This is a representative of the currently small family of caliber submachine guns .45 ASR, such as the well-known H&K UMP.



The comparison with the latter is not accidental, because the SMG-45 was created largely as a UMP response to the American market and can even use stores from it.

The creators paid great attention to the ergonomics of the weapon, providing it with two-way controls: a symmetrical fire mode switch, a magazine lock and a shutter stop. And the front handle can be used by both left-handed and right-handed alike.

Thanks to the special patented upper part, where the moving mechanism is located, the LWRC SMG-45 can be fired with the butt folded. In this case, the butt does not overlap the extractor for the sleeves. Caliber cartridge.45 ASR has a high stopping and striking ability, which makes the LWRC SMG-45 a formidable weapon.
