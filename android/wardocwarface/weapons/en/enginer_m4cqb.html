Universal submachine gun, a modification of the M16A2 with a shortened barrel 262 mm.

Purchase

The weapon does not require opening and is available in-game store from the start.

There is also a chance to obtain this weapon on time (1-7.) as a gift for daily login.

Battle performance

This gun will be the first serious replacement for the "start-up" H&K MP5. Its main drawback is low damage but good fire rate and controlled recoil is easy to compensate for this deficiency.

To the store fit 30 PP pistol cartridges that when firing quickly spent, standard ammo is 180 rounds. The weapon has a high reload speed.

For modification available in three modular slots with a small range of equipment.

Historical note

M4 Close Quarters Battle (CQB) is a versatile submachine gun, a modification of the M16A2 with a shortened barrel 262 mm.

The M4 carbines and M16 rifles are not always ideally suited to perform a variety of missions, therefore, it was proposed to use the modularity of the M16 series rifles that will enable the soldier to change the upper part of the receiver and thus to modify the weapon for a specific combat mission.

One of the two proposed solutions was the "kit" SOPMOD (Special Operations Peculiar Modification), a different solution was CQB (Close Quarters Battle).

The main objective, pursued in the development of the M4 CQB was the creation of weapons, comparable in size to a submachine gun, but firing a more powerful intermediate cartridges and assault rifles, for such tasks as the protection of VIPs, the battle in the city, and other clashes at close range (CQB).
