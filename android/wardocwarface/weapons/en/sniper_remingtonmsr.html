The MSR sniper rifle was developed by Remington Arms Holding on behalf of the U.S. Special Operations Command.

Acquisition

Weapons can be purchased for a time with credits.

Combat Effectiveness

In Warface Remington, the MSR is the longest-range bolt, allowing you to destroy enemies with a single shot. This rifle will definitely appeal to all fans of PvP!

The gold version will have a slightly increased rate of fire and an increased magazine capacity.



Several modules can be installed on the rifle, including two unique ones: the fast 4-fold Bushnell sight and the Remington MSR flame suppressor, which increases the minimum damage

Advantages

- Huge damage
- High firing range
- Good accuracy when shooting from the hip
- Three slots of modifications with a large range of modules
- Unique flame extinguisher that increases the minimum damage
- Unique fast 4x sight

Disadvantages

- No.

Historical background

The MSR (Modular Sniper Rifle) sniper rifle was created in 2009 by the famous American weapons company Remington Arms for the needs of the armed forces

Designed for high-precision predicted destruction of enemy manpower at ranges up to 1500 meters.
