Historical note

Svu-as (Sniper rifle short) is a semi — automatic sniper rifle layout "bullpup" developed TsKIB COO on the basis of the Dragunov sniper rifle. The development of a shortened sniper rifle for airborne troops, began in the 1970-ies, however, until industrial production is then not reached.

In the early 1990s, the rifle was offered the Ministry of the interior as a sniper weapon for the urban environment. MIA has attended VCA for weapons, and also demanded to develop a variant with the possibility of firing bursts.



On the accuracy of fire on small and medium range of the VCA roughly analogous to SVD. The possibility of firing bursts in a SVU-A and svu-as is provided for emergencies only (for example, in the melee, creating a real threat to the life of a sniper), because the use of a powerful cartridge and light weight of the rifle cause significant recoil when firing bursts, and a small shop does not lend itself to long shooting.

The internals of the rifle in General similar to SVD, except decisions resulting from the used layout, for example, a long pull, connecting the trigger mechanism and the trigger.

In the SVU-A and svu-as is modified, the trigger allows you to fire as a single (short press on the trigger), and queues (clicking until it stops or when you enable the ad-hoc interpreter).
