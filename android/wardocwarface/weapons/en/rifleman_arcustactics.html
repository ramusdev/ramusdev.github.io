A unique weapon that shoots dense clumps of energy. There are several variants of such blasters. This model has a balanced rate of fire, range of fire and damage.

Combat Effectiveness

Arcus series weapons can be obtained in a special launch of the Delta server.

Arcus Tactics has excellent characteristics, but the multiplicity of the sight is very strong, so it can be difficult at close distances.

Weapon Features

This model is a sniper rifle. The sight gives a five-fold increase. Effective against:

- ordinary horses at medium and long distances on any difficulty
- against the power units at the feet of the final boss.

Thanks to its high damage, huge maximum damage range, and convenient sight, this weapon is much more effective than the ARCUS Scout at a distance. And it allows you to kill ordinary people in the head at almost any distance with a single hit.

Also, the sight is very helpful to shoot back from ordinary enemies in the battle with the final boss, since the distance between the platforms is large and the submachine gun version can no longer cope.
