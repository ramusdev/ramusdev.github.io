Historical note

In 2011 the famous American company producing weapons and ammunition "Remington Arms" together with the manufacturer of upscale sports-game rifles "JP Enterprises Inc" under the program "SASS" (Semi-Automatic Sniper System) for power structures developed a new sniper rifle caliber .308 Win.

In January 2012 the new rifle, designated "R11 RSASS" (Rifle 11 Remington Semi-Automatic Sniper System rifle sample in 2011, a semi-automatic sniper system Remington) was presented to the public.



Sniper rifle R11 RSASS is built on sport platform rifle JP LRP-07. Assembly, supply and maintenance of rifles took over the firm Remington, while JP Enterprises acted as a supplier of components such as receiver, trigger mechanism and brackets for the optics.

An interesting point is that in most cases this rifle is mentioned under the name R11 RSASS that is not quite right. In fact, the weapon is referred to simply as R11, RSASS is the designation of a ready sniper kit, where a weapon is usually offered.

In this kit, in addition to the rifle, includes an optical sight Leupold Mark 4M3 variable multiplicity 4.5 to 14X, bipod Harris, the device silent shooting, and hard carrying case. br>
