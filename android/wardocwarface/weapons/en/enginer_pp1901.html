Russian submachine gun, developed in 2004 for the special forces unit of the internal troops "Vityaz". It is based on the AKS-74U and is a development of the Bison submachine gun.

Combat Effectiveness

Game advantages of the gun — controlled recoil, excellent damage and rate of fire, as well as the ability to install a unique silencer that does not reduce damage.

According to some parameters, the PP-19-01 "Vityaz" surpasses the characteristics of the AMB-17, but the advantages are compensated by a slightly reduced accuracy when shooting from the hip, as well as a smaller volume of magazine and ammunition.

Historical background

Russian-made submachine gun, developed in 2004 by the company "Izhmash". It got its name in honor of the special forces unit of the internal troops of Russia "Vityaz". Automation is based on the use of recoil energy of the free shutter.

There is no gas exhaust system, shooting is conducted from a closed bolt in single and automatic modes with 9×19 mm cartridges.
