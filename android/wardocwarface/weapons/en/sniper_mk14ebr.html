Historical note

U. S. Navy Mark 14 Enhanced Battle Rifle (EBR) is "improved military rifle" is a semi— automatic rifle, created the special operations forces of the U.S. Navy on the basis of the M14 rifle in 2004. Mk 14 EBR is used by several special units of the Navy of the United States, including the renowned division — the "SEAL" (SEa, Air, andLand).



The M14 rifle was not accidentally chosen as the basis for modification. This weapons system has proven itself in many armed conflicts involving the US army and allies, showing high accuracy of fire and reliability.

However the rifle had some obvious drawbacks — it was quite bulky because of the solid wooden box, butt, and also had a high weight of ammunition. These disadvantages and has been fixed in the Mk 14 EBR.
