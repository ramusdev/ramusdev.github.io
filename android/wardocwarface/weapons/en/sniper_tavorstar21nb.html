Historical note

Tavor STAR-21 Navy Blue — developed in 1990-ies the Israeli company Israel Military Industries and is part of a whole family of weapons under the designation "Tavor". In addition to the basic model assault rifle Tavor TAR 21 at its base were created Tavor CTAR 21 carbine, compact automatic Tavor MTAR 21, as well as a sniper rifle Tavor STAR 21.

Sniper rifle Tavor — Tavor Assault Rifle Sharpshooting 21 (STAR) is one of the most interesting versions "Tavor".

This rifle was designed to replace the armament of the Israeli Army sniper rifles of American manufacture М16А2Е3 and the like.

Like all weapons of the family, rifle Tavor STAR 21 is an automatic weapon with a gas engine automatics built bullpup configuration.

Ejector and a reflector mounted on the bolt, and the rifle includes two interchangeable version of gates providing release of spent cartridges on the right or on the left side of the weapon, which in the case of weapons made two Windows to eject spent cartridges.
