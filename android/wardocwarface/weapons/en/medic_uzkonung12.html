Combat Effectiveness

Historical background

This compact shotgun in the bullpup layout is produced by the well-known Turkish arms company Uzkon Arms Defense. Smoothbore rifles made according to the bullpup scheme are exotic in the gun market, but the UNG-12 stands out even against their background, because it uses replaceable magazines.



The body of the gun is made of aluminum (upper receiver) and plastic (lower receiver), and the barrel is made of steel grade 4140. Due to this, the weight of the gun is small: 3.75 kg. Incomplete disassembly of the shotgun (for cleaning) takes only ten seconds and does not require special tools.

The Uzkon UNG-12 immediately conquered the weapons market with its memorable design: it looks like the famous Belgian assault rifle FN F2000. In addition to the unusual appearance, this weapon is endowed with excellent combat performance, so the weapon is successfully exported to many countries of the world and is in incredible demand.
