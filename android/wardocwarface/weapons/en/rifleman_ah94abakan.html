The legendary machine gun, which allows you to fire cut-offs of two shots without recoil between them, which provides a very high accuracy of shooting. The disadvantages of the unique design of the AN-94 are the high cost of production and the complexity of maintenance.

For these reasons, this machine was produced only in small series.

Combat Effectiveness< / h4> In the game, the machine releases two bullets in semi-automatic shooting mode. The characteristics of the weapon are perfectly balanced — excellent damage and rate of fire, high aiming range and the presence of a unique handle that reduces the spread and increases the reload speed of the weapon.

Historical background

AN-94 "Abakan" - the development of the designer of small arms Gennady Nikonov. The machine gun is characterized by high accuracy and accuracy of shooting.

When firing long bursts, the weapon is not inferior to the AK-74 in accuracy and the spread of bullets, and in single-shot fire mode, it is completely superior — due to the large length of the aiming line.
