Historical note

Smoothbore automatic shotgun Auto Assault-12 (AA-12), developed in the early 70-ies in the USA designer Maxwell Atchisson. The gun was established on the basis of the earlier work "Atchisson Assault Shotgun".

The gun with enormous firepower, but at a low rate, was invented in the era of the Vietnam war because it assumed a rich sleep with buckshot and buckshot all sorts of bushes and thickets in the jungle where he was hiding opponents.

Atchison was not weapons-industrialist and in the middle of 2000-h years the American company "Military Police System" gets the rights to the AA-12, hoping to start production for the police and armed forces of the United States. Also using the ideas of Atchison in South Korea was developed automatic shotgun "USAS-12".
