Combat Effectiveness

In Warface CDX-MC Kraken, a powerful "chatter" is a successful synthesis of everything that snipers love so much: high damage, quick entry into and exit from aiming mode,increased damage to the limbs and a fairly large magazine.

In addition, the rifle has the ability to use a silencer and has a unique flame suppressor, which increases the penetration capacity more than the standard one.
