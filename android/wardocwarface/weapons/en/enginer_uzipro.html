Ultra-compact submachine gun UZI Pro

Acquisition

It is delivered for warbucks forever after opening from suppliers ("Legendary").

Combat Effectiveness

UZI Pro is already the second legendary submachine gun in the range of suppliers — all players who have accumulated a sufficient number of supply points will be able to get it.

The main advantages of the gun are the rate of fire, impressive damage multipliers on the body and limbs, comfortable recoil, good range and many unique modules.

UZI Pro is a comfortable, highly effective and perfectly balanced gun that will be appreciated by players who prefer an aggressive style of playing for an engineer.

Historical background



The ultra-compact UZI Pro submachine gun was developed by Israel Military Industries (IMI) in 2010. From its prototype, the gun is distinguished by improved ergonomics and the presence of mounts for tactical devices.

On the cover of the receiver is a Picatinny rail, which can be used to install various types of sights.
