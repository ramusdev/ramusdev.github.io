Russian sniper rifle, developed on the basis of the large-caliber sports rifle Record-CISM. The weapon uses standard cartridges of 7.62 x 54 mm caliber,which are also suitable for SVD.

Combat Effectiveness

SV-98 took the advantages of such rifles as Truvelo CMS 20x42 mm and Steyr Scout. The SV-98 has a high aiming speed and shutter twitch, and the damage to the limbs and body is increased.

It has a unique sight that increases the speed of entry into the sight, as well as a flame extinguisher that slightly increases accuracy, and a muzzle brake that slightly reduces recoil.

Historical background

The SV-98 was developed by the Izhmash concern in 1998 on the basis of the Record-CISM rifle and is designed to engage various targets at ranges of up to one kilometer. The rifle has a longitudinally sliding rotary bolt, which has three radial combat stops. The trigger mechanism of the SV-98 allows you to adjust the trigger force, position and stroke of the trigger.

The upper part of the receiver is made in the form of a Picatini bar — you can install optical sights on it. In addition, the weapon has a detachable ten-seat magazine, using standard Russian cartridges of 7.62×54 caliber.
