Historical note

Jackhammer — automatic shotgun produced by the American company "Pancor Corporation". Was developed by John Andersen in 1978, the first sample released in 1985, the first tests were conducted in the 1990-ies.

It was built only a few prototypes into service", Jackhammer" was not adopted. The weapon is a semi-automatic turret gun, made on a "bullpup" chambered 12-gauge.

The cartridges are placed in a removable cassette, made in the form of a drum.





Jackhammer is designed to arm police and army. There was also the option for arming frogmen, which differed from the standard version that had an airtight container for transporting weapons under water.

On the market of civilian weapons in most countries selling "Jackhammer is prohibited", but it is possible to shoot in many computer games.
