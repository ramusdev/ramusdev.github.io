Historical note

The smooth-bore combat shotgun USAS-12 was developed in the late 80-ies in the USA designer John Trevor, who worked for a small company "Gilbert Equipment Co". For its Foundation was taken automatic shotgun AA-12 (Auto Assault-12).



As the company "Gilbert Equipment Co" did not have its own production capacity, she began to search for manufacturer able to start production of new weapons. In the result of a long search as the manufacturer of the new rifle, designated "USAS-12", was selected as the South Korean company "Daewoo Precision Industries".
