Historical note

Unique Alpine Taktisches Präzisions Gewehr 1 (TPG-1) — tactical precision rifle developed by the Austrian company Unique Alpine. The rifle has a modular design that allows you to easily change barrels and used cartridges.

Alpine TPG-1 has a longitudinally sliding, rotating bolt. The steel receiver is rigidly mounted to the aluminum block, which in turn is attached in a detachable buttstock with pistol grip and foregrip.
