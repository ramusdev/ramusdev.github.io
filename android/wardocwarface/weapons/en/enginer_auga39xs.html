Historical note

Steyr AUG A3 9mm XS submachine gun caliber 9mm Parabellum, modification of automatic rifles Steyr Armee Universal Gewehr (AUG) - army universal rifle.

This submachine gun assembled system bullpup (where part of the barrel, slide group and shop are behind the handle fire control and trigger). Due to this, it is possible to reduce the length of the weapon while maintaining the same barrel length.

In addition, offset to the butt, the center of gravity allows the unit to quickly shift the fire front and in depth, thanks to a telescopic sight of low multiplicity. And moving the paddle to the butt has a positive effect on accuracy of fire. AUG A3 9mm XS is a very effective and reliable weapon that allows infantry platoon to adapt to the current tactical situation, the most effective in the conduct of urban warfare and in fast paced assault operations.
