Historical note

By the end of the 90s, on the basis of the layout of the Kalashnikov assault rifle was created smooth bored shotgun saiga to be a hunting cartridge 12 gauge, which is one of the most powerful models of semi-automatic weapons.

However, the saiga is applied not only to civil arrows on the competitions in practical shooting, but also is of great interest for professionals as a powerful assault weapon.

The main feature of the saiga is its layout: the earlier versions had a simple venting mechanism allow the use of only standard, 70 mm, ammunition. The gas regulator has made it possible to fire cartridges of the type "Magnum" and "promagnum", which has a much larger powder charge.
