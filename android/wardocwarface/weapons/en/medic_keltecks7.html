Historical background

The shotgun of the American weapons company Kel-Tec CNC Industries was presented in the summer of 2019 at the SHOT Show 2019 in Las Vegas. The novelty, which is based on the KSG (Kel-Tec ShotGun) model, is also built on the bullpup principle - but is thinner, more compact and lighter than the basic version, and also provides only one tubular magazine.

The full length of the smooth-bore KS7 with an 18.5-inch barrel does not exceed 663 mm. In the charged state, the KS7 is almost 0.9 kg lighter than the KSG. The shotgun has a polymer carrying handle that protects the mechanical aiming devices and provides M-LOK type mounts, a polymer handguard, a pistol grip and a receiver-butt.

All controls are two-sided: designed for both right-handed and left-handed shooters.
