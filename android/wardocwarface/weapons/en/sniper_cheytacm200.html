Historical note

CheyTac M200 "Intervention" — long-range sniper rifle, manufactured by the American company CheyTac LLC.

The rifle is part of the sniper shooting CheyTac Long Range Rifle System, and is intended, primarily, to engage at long range manpower.

The rifle is developed for special sniper ammo type Wildcat: .408 and .375 Cheyenne Tactical Cheyenne Tactical. A distinctive feature of the system is high accuracy at large distances: less than one arcminute at a distance of over 2000 m.
