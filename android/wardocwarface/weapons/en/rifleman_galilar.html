Historical note

Galil AR - the Israeli machine gun, developed by designer Yisrael Galili, on the basis of the Finnish machine Valmet Rk 62, in turn, is a licensed variant of the Kalashnikov rifle.

From the point of view of the internal structure Galil is similar to the Kalashnikov assault rifle except use a different cartridge. Automation works by removal of powder gases from the barrel through a vent hole drilled at an angle of 30 degrees back directly to the gas chamber.
