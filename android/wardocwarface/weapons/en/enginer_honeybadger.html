Historical note

Honey Badger is a modern submachine gun designed and manufactured by Advanced Armament Corporation (AAC). Weapons designed on the basis of the assault rifle AR-15 7.62×35 mm. submachine Gun was developed in close cooperation with us special forces to create a suitable and effective replacement for H&K MP5. The gun has a rifled barrel length of 150 mm. the barrel is installed and integrated a custom silencer.



Ejection of spent cartridges is on the right side of the weapon. Food arms ammunition is from detachable box magazine 30 rounds.

As a staff sighting devices used mechanical open sights. In addition, for the optical or night sight on the receiver located on top of the universal guide type Picatinny rail.

For use of additional equipment and accessories plastic handguard on the top and bottom are equipped with seats for the rails Picatinny rail. The weapon is equipped with a retractable buttstock.
