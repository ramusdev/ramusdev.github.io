Historical note

Fabarm S. A. T. 8 Pro (Semi-Automatic Tactical 8 Pro) shotgun designed and manufactured by the Italian company "FABARM" (Fabbrica Bresciana Armi). This shotgun is designed for use by officers of the policing, private security agencies and ordinary citizens (for self defense and practical shooting).

Shotgun S. A. T. 8 Pro uses the vapor-automatic located around the magazine tube, gas piston ring. Automatic weapons are highly resistant to contamination, which increases the reliability of the gun.



Rifle equipped with a patented system Pulse Piston that uses a self-regulating friction brake rollback of the mobile system to ensure reliable operation of the automation with different types of ammo. This brake consists of an elastic ring located between the gas piston and rods, leading to the bolt group.

With vigorous movement of the gas piston (when using cartridges of large capacity, with high blood pressure in the barrel), the elastic ring is compressed in the longitudinal direction, thereby increasing friction with the tube store and slowing down the rate rollback movable parts down to acceptable values.
