Historical note

Special Sniper rifle (VSS) "Vintorez" silent sniper rifle created in CRI "Tochmash" in Klimovsk in the early 1980-ies, and weapons designed for special forces. The rifle uses special ammunition SP-5 and SP-6 (armour piercing) caliber 9×39 mm SP-6 applied to the bullet core based on tungsten carbide.

The principle of operation of automation VSS Vintorez — the gases when fired. The rifle has integrated into the design of the expansion silencer with penetrazioni shock wave of the powder gases of the annular elements, diaphragms, and the trunk has a few holes to release pressure in the rear cavity of the muffler. A single exhaust sound of the shot is divided into a sound wave.

Reflected from the slanting walls of the separator, waves are superimposed on each other in opposite phases, and mutually absorbed. In addition to the optic, the rifle has an open mechanical sight — adjustable rear sight, digitized to 400 meters, and placed the muzzle of the silencer fly with protective stand.

For mounting optics provides lateral mounting type dovetail.
