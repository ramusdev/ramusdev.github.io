Unique assault rifle AK "Alpha", developed on the basis of the Kalashnikov assault rifle.

Combat Effectiveness

The AK Alpha has an easily controlled recoil and a high rate of fire. Special ammo allows you to deal increased damage to the limbs and additional damage at long range.

Also, the machine has a number of unique modules, including collimator sights Sig Sauer Romeo 4 and Hartman MH1.

Historical background

This carbine was produced by the Israeli company Command Arms & Acessories (CAA) in 2016.



It was based on the iconic Kalashnikov assault rifle of 1949, but the design was changed so that it could guess the features of three popular automatic rifles at once: the Belgian FN SCAR

American Bushmaster ACR and Italian Beretta ARX-160.



At the same time, the AK "Alpha", retained the layout of the original Kalashnikov, received a modern "body kit" and small changes in the design.

The new model has a huge combat power and has shown itself perfectly at many exhibitions and tests.
