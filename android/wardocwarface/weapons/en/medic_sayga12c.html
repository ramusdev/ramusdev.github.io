Historical note

Smoothbore shotguns "saiga" has inherited the overall layout and structure of the AK, with the gas discharging mechanism and a locking-turning the bolt.

Slide group and receiver have been redesigned based on the use of hunting cartridges, a trigger mechanism has lost self-timer, vent mechanism appeared gas regulator to ensure reliable fire like normal bullets, and the heavy duty "Magnum"rounds.



Saiga-12C — version of the gun the saiga with a folding plastic buttstock, fire selector control (like the AK) and forend hunting type.

The carbines model saiga-12C has a short barrel and a locking device firing mechanism, precluding the possibility of firing when folded.

Saiga-12S EXP-01 - the export version of the saiga-12C, the difference consists in the absence of a locking device of the trigger.
