Historical note

MSBS (Pol. Modułowy System Broni Strzeleckiej — modular system of small arms) — Polish missile platform individual weapons. Weapons designed in the result of R & d to create a modular system of small arms, with a classical layout and bullpup. br>
MSBS is designed as a platform on which manufactured rifles, hand guns and sniper rifles. MSBS is part of the Polish future soldier program — individual combat system "Titan". Modularity will simplify design, production and accelerate training soldiers in the use of weapons.



The program Modułowy System Broni Strzeleckiej kalibru 5,56 mm for the development of a new modular machine for the Polish armed forces beginning on December 4, 2007 and was originally planned to finish the development of 3 Dec 2010. The program included rifle in classic and bullpup configurations. br>
The program involved the consortium, which includes the Radom Armory and weapons technologies Institute, a division of the faculty of mechatronics and aviation Military technical Academy named after Yaroslav Dombrowski from Warsaw. 15 December 2009 new assault rifle was presented to the public in two planned options.

In 2010, established a program of MSBS in the result of the contest received the name Radon.

The family of MSBS works on the basis of energy of the powder gases drives the piston with short stroke acting on the bolt. There is a gas regulator to control the amount of gas input in the vapor tube. There are two modes: open or closed. Butterfly valve has seven lugs. The fire single or automatic.
