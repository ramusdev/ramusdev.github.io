Sighting system has day, night and conventional optical channels, laser rangefinder and ballistic computer for grenade launcher.

Automatic ARX-160 is based on removal of powder gases from the bore, gas piston has a short stroke. Has a slide catch. Unusual for this class of weapons is the ability to quickly change barrel in the field without disassembly of the weapon.

Switching the direction of the release liners is carried out by pressing a bullet or other sharp hard object on a special cross-pin, located at the rear of the receiver.

The result of the movement of the pin on either side of two symmetrically arranged in the breech of the ejector-reflector changing its functions, providing release liners in the right direction.

Fuse-translator fire mode is located on both sides of the receiver and provides maintenance of single and automatic fire.
