Historical note

ACR CQB — shortened version of the assault rifle the ACR, developed in the US in 2007, the company Magpul Industries Corporation and produced to this day.

Developed in record time (4 months) project Masada originally modernized appearance rifles family AR15 and M16. However, in the process the developers went further than expected. Instead of the banal updates of the popular, but outdated rifle was created brand new!

ACR CQB easy to appeal as under the right and under the left arm, which is a big advantage compared to the M16.
