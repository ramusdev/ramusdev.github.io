Historical note

Sniper rifle system Evgeniya Dragunova (SVD), adopted by the Soviet Army in 1963. Automatic rifle operates due to the removal of powder gases through a side opening in the barrel wall. Especially for SVD was created "sniper" cartridge with a bullet with steel core, but the rifle can use all types of Russian ammo 7.62h54r.

All variants of the Dragunov rifle has a removable open sighting device in the form of flies in the namushnike and adjustable rear sight, located in front of the cover of the receiver. Bracket type "Dovetail" for an optical sight mounted to the receiver on the left.

Initially, the forearm and the butt of frame construction was made of wood, to more modern versions these parts are made of plastic. One of the characteristic features of the SVD — presence of stem the tide for mounting a bayonet-knife.
