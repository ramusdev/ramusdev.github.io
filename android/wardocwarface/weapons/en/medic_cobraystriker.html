Historical note

Protecta — smooth-bore shotgun combat 12-gauge (about 18.5 mm), built by the revolving scheme (known to players as Cobray Striker). This gun was designed by Hilton Walker in the early 1980s.

After the fall of his home state of Rhodesia, Hilton moved to South Africa, where he continued to work on the weapon.

The first combat examples of "Protecta" in the mid 80s entered service with police and military units and was intended for operations that required more firepower at short distances, mainly in urban environments.
