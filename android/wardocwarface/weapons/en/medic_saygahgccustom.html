Historical note

The first carbine family of saiga appeared in the 70-ies. The impetus for its creation was an appeal by the first Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist party of Kazakhstan D. A. Kunaev to the General Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee L. I. Brezhnev with the request for the creation of weapons with which one could produce the ejection of the saiga.

The fact that migratory saiga ate and trampled the major crops, and hunting parties, armed with smooth-bore hunting rifles were not able to deal with animals. Then the designers of "IZHMASH" started creating hunting carbines saiga.



Four years, the designers and testers of the group, together with representatives of "the ÌGLAVOHOTAÎ" and local hunters have experienced rifles and brought them to perfection, mainly in Kazakhstan. After the development of new weapons was made about 300 carbines models saiga, chambered for the 5.6×39 mm.

Although in the 1970-ies was the first commercial batch of self-loading hunting carbines chambered for 5.6×39 mm carbine many years remained rare.

By the end of the 90s, on the basis of the layout of the Kalashnikov assault rifle was created and a smoothbore carbine "saiga" under a hunting cartridge 12 gauge, which is one of the most powerful models of semi-automatic weapons.

Interestingly, the "saiga 12" in model "030" applies not only to civil arrows on the competitions in practical shooting, but also is of great interest for professionals as a powerful assault weapons. In the combat variant called the carbine "18.5 COP-K".
