Purchase

Hawk Pump can be purchased in the game store ("Arms") for Warbucks after its opening from suppliers ("Rare").

Battle performance

The main advantage of Hawk Pump before competitors - balanced. He's not as lethal as the SPAS-12 is not as rapid-fire as the Remington 870 CB, but this is its main advantage — it is the "Golden mean".

The chance to kill with one shot higher than the Remington 870 CB, and the rate usually allows you to make a second shot in case of a miss, or insufficient damage. Perfect accuracy "from the hip" allows you to fight even at medium range.

If you think SPAS-12 is too slow, and Remington 870 CB insufficiently lethal, Hawk Pump — your choice.

The use of this weapon is allowed in tournaments Warface.
