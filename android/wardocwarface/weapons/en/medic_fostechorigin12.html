The Origin-12 self-loading smoothbore rifle is developed and manufactured by a small American weapons company, Fostech Outdoors.

The gun is based on the design of the Russian Saiga-12 rifle, but almost all the design elements have been revised and reworked.

Compared to the prototype, the American rifle has significantly improved ergonomics and changed the design of the gas outlet unit. Also introduced is a quick-change barrel, which allows you to change not only its length, but also the caliber of the weapon.

Acquisition

It can be purchased in the game store for credits for a certain period of time (1 h, 1 d., 7 d., 30 d.) or forever.

Combat Effectiveness

According to the game parameters, the Origin-12 has a high rate of fire, good damage and good shooting accuracy, including at long distances.

In addition, the shotgun boasts an impressive drum for 20 rounds, which also reloads quickly. The golden version of the weapon will not only set you apart from other fighters, but will also have an increased pace and range of fire.

From the modules for installation, a sight and a flame extinguisher are available. The sum of these characteristics not only makes Origin-12 an ideal option for PvE-game modes, but also allows you to feel comfortable in PvP. You can get this shotgun from the boxes of good luck for credits.
