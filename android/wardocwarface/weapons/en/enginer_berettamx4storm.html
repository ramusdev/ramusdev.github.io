Historical note

Beretta MX4 Storm — a rapid-fire submachine gun designed by the renowned Italian company "Beretta". MX4 was presented at the international exhibition IDEX 2011, where he announced his winning of the tender, the Indian Ministry of 34 500 units of this weapon.

Submachine gun Beretta Mx4 is designed on the basis of a self-loading carbine Beretta Cx4 Storm. The main difference from the prototype made for the civilian market weapons is the possibility of firing a continuous line.



Plastic case gun Beretta Mx4 is a single piece with the butt and pistol grip, thanks to the use of lightweight polymers, PP is very easy to use. Despite the fact that the butt is made of plastic, it is durable enough for use in the melee.

Sights consist of open mechanical and rail Picatinny rail between them, to which can be attached various collimator and optical sights.
