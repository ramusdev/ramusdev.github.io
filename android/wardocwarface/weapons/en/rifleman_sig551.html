Historical note

SIG 551 assault rifle, developed by German company SIG Sauer GmbH in 1983 in Switzerland.

Requirements for the new machine was put forward by the leadership of the armed forces of Switzerland in 1978.

After successful testing, the SIG 551, the military leadership in 1983, announces the adoption of it on Board, however, due to financial difficulties, production was established only in the second half of the decade, and the official adoption took place in 1990.
