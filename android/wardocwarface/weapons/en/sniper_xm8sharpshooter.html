Historical note

XM8 — a complex of small arms, developed jointly by the American arms company and Alliant Techsystems German Heckler & Koch-based rifles H&K G36.

HM8 is mainly developed to replace the assault rifles are the M16 and M4A1. When using new ammunition with a lightweight composite plastic casing the complex provides weight savings of 20% compared to the M16.

The receiver and forend made of durable plastic. The butt is adjustable along the length of the telescoping five-position, easily replaceable, also made of plastic, and the trunk has a high survivability (more than 20 000 shots).

Modular configuration allows the use of barrels of different lengths, fitting weapons under specific conditions.

Possible configurations: Carbine — the carbine uses a barrel length of 318 mm;Short Carbine and PDW short barrel rifle and personal defense weapon of the crew of combat vehicles, respectively. Use trunks with a length of 229 mm; Automatic Rifle and Sharpshooter — automatic and sniper rifles that use a barrel length of 508 mm; LMG — a light machine gun that uses a weighted barrel length of 508 mm.
