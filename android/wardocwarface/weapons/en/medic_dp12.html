Pump-action shotgun the DP-12 designed and manufactured by the American arms company Standard Manufacturing. This is a highly unusual shotgun designed to combine all the advantages, but also inevitably and disadvantages of two different systems – the double-barrelled guns and store guns with pump action reloading.

This weapon can be used both for hunting and for protection and recreational shooting.
